    Eyecare for all.

    The Snellen eye chart is a universal symbol at optomoligist’s worldwide where eyecare is readily provided. This poster aims to communicate a universal need for eyecare with the eye catching headline EYECARE FOR ALL.

    By appealing to the masses, this poster calls for those with access to eyecare to donate to World Sight Day 2019 and provide to those who don't have the opportunity to readily access visioncare.
